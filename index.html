The Whole Story

We want to show the world what a real donut tastes like!

We are a small family-owned shop, opened with the sole goal of creating a bakery free of harmful ingredients. Today, as then, we focus on the underserved—people with gluten, dairy, egg, and soy sensitivities, the health-minded, and, most importantly, allergic kids who are often unable to indulge. We select all ingredients responsibly and use our natural sweeteners sparingly. Our goal is to make eating vegan and gluten-free fun and delicious, and we’re very glad to have you with us.
